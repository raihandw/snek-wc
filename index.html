Meet Our Contestants

Lu Tran "The Venom" Nguyen

King Cobra (Ophiophagus hannah)

Vietnam

The legendary Lu Tran "The Venom" Nguyen was known for his deadly venom. The former reigning champion is said to have a count kill of 1097 humans! The fact that no one decided that this snake might be a bit too dangerous to be kept alive only proofs how dangerous (and profitable) he is. This year he's back and is determined to retake the crown from Silva "Gigante" Mendes.

Neela "Queen Bolt" Behati

Black Mamba (Dendroaspis polylepis)

Ethiopia

The newcomer from the heartlands of Africa, Neela "Queen Bolt" Behati is the fastest snake in Africa, and perhaps, the world! She is so fast that we don't know where she is right now. If you see her, please kindly and calmly leave the perimeter, she is deadly! Fear not however, because she is determined to beat all the legends in the stage, assuming if we found her that is.

Silva "Gigante" Mendes

Green Anaconda (Eunectes murinus)

Brazil

Hailing from the not-so-huge-due-to-deforestation amazonian jungle, the reigning champion is here to defend its title. What he lacks in anything that involves speed, he excels in his raw strength and verosity. Last year, it was rumored that he slapped an african rhino so hard that their entire species becomes endangered. How did he ended up slapping rhinos in africa? Who knows! All we know is he's ready to slap some more!
